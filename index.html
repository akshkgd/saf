Quality standards

We take every step to assure you of the highest level of freshness and hygiene:

  • Directly sourced from farms through our collection centers across India
  • Daily fresh produce ensured
  • Sorted and graded twice by trained personnel
  • Sourced from production hubs
  • No use of harmful ripening agents
  • Regular development programs for farmers and suppliers are carried out to improve skills and quality
  • Fruits and vegetables are well-below the standard Maximum Residue Level or Maximum Residue Limit(MRL) which is the maximum permissible amount of pesticide residue that should be left behind in a crop for safe consumption
